🔑 one-time setup

takes ~2 minutes • free forever • no credit card

1
Go to console.firebase.google.com and sign in with any Google account.
2
Click "Add project" → give it any name (e.g. hangout-planner) → disable Google Analytics → click "Create project".
3
In the left sidebar: Build → Realtime Database → Create Database → pick any region → choose "Start in test mode" → click Enable.
4
You'll see your database URL at the top — it looks like:
https://hangout-planner-a1b2c-default-rtdb.firebaseio.com Copy that whole URL.
5
Paste it below 👇 to generate the line you need for config.js.
